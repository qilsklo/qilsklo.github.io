I write my writings upon this writ;
Perhaps I'll show some sense of wit.
Shakespeare and Frost I loved all the while,
But Joyce and Tolstoy are more my style.
This bit of my brain which I keep on refrain,
I have published here for world's fame.
Soon you'll see it too
That the thoughts I lay upon here—
Just might ring true.
